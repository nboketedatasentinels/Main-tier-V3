# Partners Guide

This guide supports partner organizations managing cohorts and reporting outcomes.

## Program Setup

- **Confirm organization profile**: ensure the company name, code, and cohort identifiers are set.
- **Assign partner contacts**: designate primary contacts who can coordinate with admins.
- **Publish timelines**: set start dates, kickoff sessions, and milestone checkpoints.

## Cohort Operations

- **Invite participants** through managed onboarding lists.
- **Track participation** via weekly engagement summaries.
- **Escalate blockers** to admins if learners are locked out or suspended.

## Reporting & Insights

- Review engagement trends (logins, completions, feedback).
- Align results to partner objectives and deliverables.
- Request reconciliation reports after migrations to confirm data accuracy.

## Communication Tips

- Send weekly reminders before live sessions.
- Share success stories to reinforce adoption.
- Provide a clear escalation path for support needs.
