# Learners Guide

This guide helps learners onboard, navigate dashboards, and make the most of the platform.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign in** using your email and password.
2. **Complete onboarding** on the welcome screen to personalize your experience.
3. **Select your goals** so the platform can recommend relevant journeys.

## Core Areas

### Dashboard

- **Weekly Glance** shows your progress, upcoming sessions, and active journeys.
- **Points & Streaks** reflect completed activities and engagement.
- **Recommendations** highlight the next best action based on your goals.

### Journeys

- Enroll in a journey that matches your role and goals.
- Track milestones in each journey and celebrate completion.

### Community

- Participate in cohort discussions to connect with peers.
- Share reflections and recognize others' contributions.

## Tips for Success

- Log in weekly to keep your streak active.
- Complete reflection prompts right after sessions.
- Use the dashboard filters to focus on your current priorities.

## Troubleshooting

- If you are redirected to onboarding, finish or skip the onboarding flow to proceed.
- Contact support if you see an account status message that blocks access.
