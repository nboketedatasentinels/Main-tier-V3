# FAQ

## Access & Login

**Why am I redirected to onboarding after login?**
- Your onboarding status is incomplete. Finish or skip onboarding to proceed.

**Why do I see an account status error?**
- Your account may be inactive or suspended. Contact support for assistance.

## Journeys & Progress

**How do I reset my streak?**
- Streaks reset automatically after inactivity. Focus on completing weekly activities.

**Where can I see my points?**
- Points are visible on the dashboard and weekly glance card.

## Partner & Admin Questions

**How do we verify migrations?**
- Run the reconciliation script and review the generated report.

**What should we do if migration data mismatches?**
- Fix the source data, rerun the migration or reconciliation, and document the outcome.
