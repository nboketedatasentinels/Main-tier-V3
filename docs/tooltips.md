# Product Tooltips

Use these tooltips for consistent copy across dashboards and admin workflows.

## Learner Dashboard

- **Weekly Glance**: "Your quick snapshot of weekly goals, progress, and upcoming sessions."
- **Points Earned**: "Points reflect completed activities and engagement this week."
- **Active Journey**: "Your current journey and the next milestone to complete."
- **Streak**: "Keep your streak alive by completing at least one activity each week."

## Journeys

- **Journey Progress**: "Track your milestones and see what's next."
- **Reflection Prompt**: "Capture your learning right after each session."

## Admin Dashboards

- **Assigned Organizations**: "Organizations you can manage and report on."
- **Account Status**: "Controls user access: active, inactive, pending, or suspended."
- **Transformation Tier**: "Defines the learner experience and available dashboards."
- **Migration Status**: "Shows whether migration updates have been applied to a profile."
